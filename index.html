Memo Cat

Memo Cat is a personal automation application designed to upload videos to YouTube on behalf of the authenticated user using Google OAuth 2.0.

The application is used only for managing and automating video uploads. It does not access, store, sell, or share any personal user data. Access is restricted to the authorized Google account only.

Purpose of the Application

Memo Cat helps content creators automate YouTube video uploads securely while maintaining full control over their Google account permissions.
